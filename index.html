About Prividium

A full ZK-based blockchain that keeps data private within your infrastructure.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Prividium.

Does anchoring to Ethereum expose private data?

No. Only proofs are shared — never transaction data.

Is Prividium another permissioned ledger?

No. ZK proofs replace consortium trust models, with verifiable execution and public settlement guarantees.

How does security hold with a single operator?

Invalid blocks fail verification automatically. Ethereum and ZKsync enforce correctness at the settlement layer.

Is selective disclosure enough for regulators?

Yes. Regulators and auditors can verify exactly what they need, backed by cryptographic proofs instead of manual reconciliations.

How does Prividium scale?

Prividium can scale horizontally with multiple instances and vertically by optimizing circuit design and proving infrastructure.
