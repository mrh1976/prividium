Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Prividium.

Does anchoring to Ethereum expose private data?

No. Only ZK proofs and minimal metadata are posted on Ethereum. These attest to correctness of state transitions without revealing underlying transaction details or balances.

Is Prividium just another permissioned ledger?

Prividium uses cryptographic proofs rather than consortium trust. Every batch is verifiable by anyone, while execution and data stay within your environment.

How can security hold if there is a single operator?

The operator controls ordering and policies, but cannot finalize invalid blocks. Any incorrect state will fail verification, so results are constrained by the proof system instead of operator behavior.

Is selective disclosure enough for regulators?

Selective disclosure lets regulators independently verify balances, flows, or specific fields when they are ready, while institutions keep full ledgers private.

How does Prividium handle throughput and scale?

A single Prividium chain can handle high throughput, and enterprises can deploy multiple chains in parallel that interoperate, scaling horizontally as demand grows.
