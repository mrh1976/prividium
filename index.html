Prividium enables new use cases

Private execution with public trust,
built for institutional workflows.

From intraday liquidity to cross-entity workflows, Prividium lets institutions modernize core operations without giving up privacy, compliance, or control.

Real-time intraday settlement

Move collateral, cash, and positions instantly instead of waiting on batch windows and T+1 rails. Treasury and trading teams see finality in minutes, while underlying data stays private inside your infrastructure.

Cross-entity workflows

Coordinate across legal entities, desks, or partners on a single shared ledger. Each party sees only what they are entitled to, while ZK proofs certify that every step executed correctly.

Compliance-aware automation

Bake KYC, limits, and approvals directly into onchain logic. Role-based permissions and selective disclosure let compliance teams validate behavior without exposing confidential flows or counterparties.

Tokenized markets with control

Issue and manage tokenized assets, run auctions, or operate private markets with Ethereum-grade security. Market structure can evolve, but the rules of the system remain transparent and verifiable.
